Frequently Asked Questions

How does Date Drop work?

Sign up, fill out our in-depth questionnaire about your values and interests, and opt-in weekly. We curate a highly compatible match for you each week and deliver it straight to your inbox.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our algorithm prioritizes long-term compatibility factors like core values, lifestyle preferences, and relationship goals, rather than just surface-level attributes.

What do you do with my data?

Your data is kept strictly confidential and is used solely for the purpose of finding your best matches. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Who can join?

Currently, Date Drop is exclusively available for UQ students.

What measures are taken to ensure safety?

We require an active @uq.edu.au email address for registration to verify that all members are genuine students within the university community.

Does it cost money?

Nope. Date Drop is completely free!

Is this for non-romantic relationships?

Yes! During sign-up, choose if you're looking for friends, dates, or both.

How is friend matching different?

Friend matching emphasizes shared hobbies, academic interests, and activity preferences to help you build a meaningful platonic connection.

What if I'm busy?

No problem! You can easily pause your account or skip weeks whenever you don't have time to meet someone new.

Does it work?

Absolutely. We've facilitated thousands of successful matches that have led to lasting friendships and relationships across campus.
